About Me

Hi everyone, I'm Nimay, a relitistic photographer based in India. My journey way back in 2022 began with a simple mobile camera and a passion for capturing moments. Over the years, I've honed my skills and developed a unique style that blends natural light with candid expressions. When I'm not behind the lens, you can find me exploring new places or experimenting with new techniques. The catch is that all my photos uploaded here yet are taken from a good mobile phone only. Feel free to reach out for collaborations or just to connect! Hope you enjoy browsing through my photographs.
