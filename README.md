🌿 About Me

Hi, I’m Nimay — a realistic photographer based in India.

My journey began in 2022 with just a mobile camera and a curiosity to capture everyday moments. Over time, this curiosity evolved into a deeper appreciation for light, emotion, and storytelling.

My work focuses on:

Natural light
Candid expressions
Quiet, meaningful moments

I believe photography is not just about capturing images, but preserving feelings that words often cannot express.
